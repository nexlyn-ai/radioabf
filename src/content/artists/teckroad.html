Teckroad (real name Patrick Karlson) is a French DJ, producer, and electronic music enthusiast born in 1967. With a career spanning decades, he embodies the spirit of long-time passion for dance music, from the disco era to modern electronic productions.

Early career & DJ roots

Patrick Karlson began DJing in the 1980s (around the mid-to-late 80s), during the golden age of analog mixing with iconic Technics SL-1200 MK2 turntables. He was active as a club and party DJ from the 1980s to the 2000s, navigating through evolving styles including disco, funk, acid house, new beat, house, garage, trance, psy-trance, progressive, and early techno influences. This broad exposure shaped his eclectic taste and deep love for groove-oriented electronic sounds.

After a period away from active DJing, he returned to the scene in the late 2010s / early 2020s, embracing digital production and online platforms to share his music and mixes.

Productions & style

Under the alias Teckroad, Patrick focuses on tech house, house, trance, psytrance, and electronic productions. His tracks often feature groovy basslines, atmospheric builds, and a nostalgic yet fresh vibe. Notable releases available on SoundCloud include:

  • Astral Injection - Univers (Original Extended Mix)
  • Astral Injection - The Beginning (Original Mix)

He also produces remixes and edits, particularly in the 80s/90s retro style, as seen in his long-running series "My RMX 80 90'S" (with episodes reaching over 292 by 2023).

His sound blends classic house/techno roots with contemporary energy, making him a versatile artist suited for club sets, online radio collaborations, and psytrance explorations.

Collaborations & media presence

Teckroad is active on platforms like SoundCloud (user Teckroad), Mixcloud, and has collaborated with international DJs. Highlights include:

  • B2B Psytrance Mix with DJ Darkness (Japan) — released in January 2024, a high-energy psytrance back-to-back session shared via Mixcloud, showcasing his ability to adapt to faster, psychedelic styles.
  • Guest mixes and features on shows like Betty Mix (Tech House collaboration in 2023).

He maintains a low-key but dedicated online presence, regularly uploading mixes and originals that attract fans of retro-futuristic electronic music.

Quote often linked to his vibe: « I always liked the music... from disco to psy! »

A true veteran of the electronic scene, Teckroad (Patrick Karlson) bridges the analog 80s/90s era with today's digital production world, still releasing fresh tracks and mixes in 2026. Passionate, consistent, and forever groovy.
