ABF CLUB // SATURDAYS GUEST MIX

 

Lex Green is a German DJ and producer born and raised in Munich, now based in Switzerland (near Ermatingen) for several years. Passionate about electronic music since the 1990s, he is recognized for his sets and productions in house, deep house, tech house, melodic house & techno, and broader club-oriented groovy and atmospheric sounds.

Beginnings & influences

Lex Green discovered electronic music in the 90s in Munich, inspired by the resident house DJs of the era and further shaped by his trips to Ibiza, where the unique nightlife atmosphere left a lasting mark. That Ibiza lifestyle remains a constant source of inspiration for his mixes and tracks.

He honed his skills by completing an advanced DJ course at the Ministry of Sound in Switzerland, under the guidance of Johnathan Dj Tutor (Ellaskins).

Radio career & residencies

Lex Green is a regular on international airwaves, with residencies on several prestigious webradios:

  • Ibiza Live Radio (ES)
  • Radio Beachgrooves (ES)
  • Estacionibizaradio (CO)
  • Digital X Radio (DE)
  • Club Sabroso Radio (DO)
  • Under Blue Radio (AR)
  • And more recently We Get Lifted Radio (UK – first Saturday of the month)

He hosts regular shows, features guest mixes from other DJs, and promotes "The Finest in House" worldwide.

Productions & discography

As a producer, Lex Green releases on respected labels, often featured in melodic house & techno, tech house, afro house, and progressive compilations. Notable appearances include:

  • Remixes and collaborations on Bonzai Progressive (e.g., "U-Boot" remix with Emanuel (D), "Crazy Town")
  • Tracks in compilations like Melodic House & Techno Trax, Afro House Selections, Tech House Sureplayers

His releases are available on Beatport, Spotify (≈134 monthly listeners), SoundCloud, Mixcloud, and YouTube, where he shares live sessions and series such as "The Finest in House & Deep House".

Style & presence

Lex Green’s sound is groovy, elegant, and dancefloor-ready: a blend of deep house, punchy tech house, atmospheric melodic techno, and Ibiza-inspired vibes. His sets are immersive, perfect for clubs, radio, or on-the-go listening.

Very active in 2026, he continues to release fresh mixes, collaborate, and grow his global network from Switzerland.

Quote often linked to his vibe: « The finest in house — from Munich to Ibiza, the journey never ends! »

A passionate ambassador of European house music, Lex Green brings groove, depth, and positive energy to every set, connecting the roots of the 90s with today’s contemporary scene.
