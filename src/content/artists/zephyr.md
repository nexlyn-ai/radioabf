						<h2><strong>ABF CLUB [ MADE IN JAPAN ] // Every Sunday 20h CET (= 19h UTC)</strong></h2><p>&nbsp;</p><p><strong>Zephyr</strong> (also known as <strong>Zephyr (Jpn)</strong> or <strong>DJ Zephyr (Jpn)</strong>, real name <strong>Hitomi Nagayama</strong>) is a <strong>Japanese DJ, producer</strong>, and <strong>house music enthusiast</strong> based in <strong>Japan</strong> (with mentions of activity in Tokyo/Yūrakuchō).</p><p>A dedicated lover of <strong>house music</strong> since the early days of the genre, Hitomi draws heavy inspiration from pioneers like <strong>Mark Moore</strong>, <strong>Tim Simenon</strong>, <strong>Jimmy Cauty</strong>, <strong>Bill Drummond</strong>, and <strong>Boy George</strong>. His sound is rooted in <strong>deep house</strong>, <strong>melodic house</strong>, <strong>techno-infused house</strong>, and <strong>progressive</strong> vibes, often featuring atmospheric builds, groovy baselines, and emotional depth.</p><h3>Career &amp; productions</h3><p>Active in the international electronic scene (particularly online and radio-focused), Zephyr has built a presence through regular guest mixes, live broadcasts, and original productions.
