ABF CLUB // SATURDAYS GUEST MIX


MG Gibson (real name Marcus Gibson) is an American DJ passionate about underground sounds, based in the United States. Known for his high-energy sessions and deep love for funky house, jackin' house, minimal house, tech house, and authentic underground vibes, he has built a loyal following through his consistent groove-driven mixes.

Career & style

MG Gibson hosts his flagship show Gibson's Underground Sessions, broadcast every Sunday at 8:00 PM EST, with replays and archives available on YouTube. His shows are pure celebrations of funky and underground house, featuring fresh tracks, personal edits, and selections that keep the energy high without compromise. He regularly drops special sessions (birthdays, New Year, techno collabs...) that bring surprise guests and extra fire to the mix.

His sound is funky, deep, and relentlessly dancefloor-focused: solid basslines, catchy vocals, minimal touches, and an energy that never drops. He excels at long, immersive sets that feel like a real club night — perfect for dedicated house heads seeking true underground flavor.

Media presence & activities

MG Gibson shares his mixes and updates on YouTube (personal uploads and archives), Facebook (@mg.gibson.54), and Instagram (@marcusgibsonktv), where he interacts with fans, announces shows, and posts replays.

Still very active in 2026, MG Gibson continues dropping weekly sessions, special editions, and surprise collaborations, staying true to his motto: GOD FAMILY DJING.
