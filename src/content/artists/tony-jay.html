Tony Jay is a French DJ, producer, and electronic music selector based in Paris, France. Known for his energetic and versatile sets, he specializes in house, tech house, deep house, funky house, and club-oriented electronic sounds, with a strong emphasis on groove, vocal elements, and dancefloor impact.

Career & style

Tony Jay has been active in the French electronic scene for several years, building his reputation through club performances, residencies, and regular guest appearances on webradios. His style is characterized by uplifting, groovy, and positive vibes: seamless transitions between current house anthems, funky edits, deep basslines, and well-timed vocal drops that keep the crowd moving non-stop.

He excels at blending modern club tracks with timeless house classics, making his sets accessible yet sophisticated — perfect for peak-time slots, pre-parties, or extended radio sessions.

Radio & online presence

Tony Jay is a frequent contributor to international webradios, including:

  • Radio ABF (where he delivers resident mixes in the ABF Club concept)
  • Other platforms like Radio FG, JDK Radio, or We Get Lifted Radio (guest slots)

His mixes are shared on Mixcloud (under profiles like Tony Jay or related aliases), SoundCloud, and social media, often featuring tracklists and high-energy live recordings. He also collaborates with other DJs for B2B sessions, showcasing his adaptability and chemistry on the decks.

Activities & vibe

Still very active in 2026, Tony Jay continues to produce edits, share fresh mixes, and perform in clubs and online events. His sound is all about feel-good energy, groove, and pure dancefloor connection — no unnecessary complexity, just pure party fuel.

Quote often linked to his vibe: « Groove is the key — let's keep the floor moving! »

A reliable and passionate figure in the Parisian electronic scene, Tony Jay brings fresh house vibes, club-ready selections, and unstoppable positivity to every set and release.
