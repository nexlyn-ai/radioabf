DJ Pierre de Paris is a French electronic music DJ, producer, and podcast founder based in Paris, France. Specializing in electro, deep house, house, and underground electronic sounds, he is known for his atmospheric, groovy sets that blend modern club energy with classic influences.

Career & style

Pierre de Paris emerged in the electronic scene as a dedicated selector and creator of immersive mixes. He is the founder of ELECTRO RADIO PODCAST, a platform where he shares regular episodes featuring his signature selections of electro, deep house, and house tracks. He also founded the UNDER THE GROUND collective, a group focused on promoting underground electronic music through events, mixes, and collaborations.

His DJ style emphasizes deep, hypnotic, and dancefloor-oriented grooves, often incorporating electro elements, subtle builds, and a sophisticated touch that appeals to both clubbers and home listeners. His sets are energetic yet refined, making him a fitting contributor to the vibrant Paris electronic underground.

Media presence & activities

Pierre de Paris maintains an active online presence, primarily through:

  • Facebook (PIERRE.DE.PARIS.dj): Where he announces mixes, podcasts, and events (with a growing community of fans)
  • Podcast platforms for ELECTRO RADIO PODCAST: Regular drops of full-length episodes showcasing his productions and curations

He continues to be active in 2026, releasing fresh podcast episodes, sharing mixes, and supporting the underground scene through his collective. His work bridges the classic Paris electronic heritage with contemporary sounds, making him a respected figure in the city's webradio and independent electronic circles.
