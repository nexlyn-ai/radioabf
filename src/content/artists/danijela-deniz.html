ABF CLUB // SATURDAYS GUEST MIX

 

Danijela Deniz (also known as Danijela(Deniz) or Danijela Deniz Stojanovic) is an international female DJ, producer, remixer, video-media artist, and electronic music creator based internationally (with strong ties to online global platforms). She is a versatile artist known for her work in psytrance, techno, afro house, trance, and other high-energy electronic genres.

Career & style

Danijela Deniz is a multi-talented creator who produces original tracks, remixes, mashups, reworks, and full DJ sets, often accompanied by complete media art-videos, flyers, and visual content. Her sound spans hypnotic psytrance journeys, driving techno vibes, summery afro house grooves, and uplifting trance mixes — always aiming to evoke strong emotions and make listeners feel like they're in a club, even from home.

She has collaborated as a featured vocalist on several releases, including:

  • Young Bad Twinz – "We Around There" (feat. Danijela Deniz, Subwoofer Records)
  • Young Bad Twinz – "Y, Ome" (feat. Danijela Deniz, Subwoofer Records)
  • Young Bad Twinz – "Bea Boutit Yall" (feat. Danijela Deniz)
  • Other tracks like "Yo, Yo Turn It Up" (feat. Danijela Deniz)

Her productions and remixes (e.g., "The Distance & Igi - Wanderer (Rework 2020)") are available on SoundCloud, Beatport, Spotify, Apple Music, Deezer, and Mixcloud.

Radio & online presence

Danijela Deniz is a regular contributor to international webradios, with a prominent weekly slot on KTV RADIO (Montreal-based online station) — broadcasting her mixes weekdays at 8am EST. She shares full sets on YouTube (e.g., "Afro House Mix SUMMER FEELS", "PSY TRANCE MIX COLONY", "Techno Mix HYSTERIA", "Trance Mix WAVES OF WONDER") and platforms like Mixcloud (under Danijela Deniz Stojanovic / danijela-deniz-lekic), where fans can discover her psytrance, techno, and house sessions.

She is also linked to Monkey Forest Records (via Facebook) and promotes her work through dedicated artist pages.

Still very active in 2026, Danijela Deniz continues to release new mixes, remixes, and visual-media projects, connecting with a global audience through her passionate, emotion-driven electronic music.

Quote often linked to her vibe: « Music is what feelings sound like out loud. »

A dynamic international force in electronic music, Danijela Deniz blends production, DJing, vocals, and visual art to create immersive, club-ready experiences — from psytrance shadows to afro house summer feels.
