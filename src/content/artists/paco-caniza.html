ABF CLUB // SATURDAYS GUEST MIX

 

Paco Caniza is a French-Spanish DJ, producer, and label CEO based in Paris, France. Of Spanish origin and born in Paris, he is a passionate advocate of house music with deep roots in disco, funk, and classic house sounds.

Career & beginnings

Paco Caniza started his DJ career mixing in Spanish clubs and regularly appearing on English, Brazilian, and U.S. radio shows. He began producing in 2012, quickly gaining recognition when Lucas Reyes invited him to host a weekly residency on Bahia Music Radio every Friday on Radio FG USA — a major boost that helped him climb the ranks in the international house scene.

His productions soon caught attention, with tracks charting on platforms like Beatport and Traxsource, appearing in newsletters, home banners, and "must-hear" selections. Supported and played by prominent DJs such as Umek, StoneBridge, Lenny Fontana, Chuckie, Crazibiza, Black Legend, and many more, Paco established himself as a reliable force in the house community.

Productions & labels

Paco Caniza has released extensively on respected labels including:

  • Jango Music (exclusive artist for a period)
  • Pornostar Records (Crazibiza label)
  • Disco Ball Records
  • Whore House Records
  • Nu Monkey Records
  • Weekend Warriors
  • Robotical Grooves
  • Club Session
  • Re:vibe Music
  • Pina Colada Records
  • And many others

His discography features groovy, dancefloor-oriented house tracks like "Disco Beat", "House Religion", "What You Waiting For?", "Waiting For You", "Escape From All I Know", "Moving On Up" (ft. Phie Claire), "With Me", and numerous originals/remixes in jackin house, funky house, vocal house, and disco-influenced styles.

He is also the CEO of the house music label Panam Vibes Records (often stylized @panam-vibes-records), through which he supports emerging talent and continues to shape the genre.

Rankings & presence

His music is available on Beatport, Traxsource, Spotify (≈5.9K monthly listeners), SoundCloud (@paco-caniza), Apple Music, TIDAL, and more. He remains active with regular mixes (e.g., Luvin House vocal/funky/jazzy/deep/soulful series), new releases, and collaborations into 2025–2026.

Quote often linked to his vibe: « House music is my religion — groovy, funky, and forever on the dancefloor. »

A dedicated house music ambassador bridging French, Spanish, and international scenes, Paco Caniza delivers timeless grooves, jackin energy, and pure disco soul through his productions, DJ sets, and label work. Still climbing the ladder with passion and consistency!
