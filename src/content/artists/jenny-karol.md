						<h2>ABF CLUB // SATURDAYS GUEST MIX</h2><p>&nbsp;</p><p><strong>Jenny Karol</strong> (stylized as <strong>Jenny Karol ॐ</strong>) is an <strong>international DJ, programmer</strong>, <strong>music producer</strong>, and <strong>label founder</strong> specializing in <strong>trance</strong>, <strong>uplifting trance</strong>, <strong>progressive trance</strong>, <strong>tech trance</strong>, <strong>psytrance</strong>, and <strong>emotional psy</strong> sounds. She is the <strong>CEO, Founder, and Owner</strong> of <strong>AVIVmedia</strong>, a digital music label dedicated to promoting trance and psytrance artists worldwide.</p><h3>Career &amp; radio presence</h3><p>Jenny Karol began her DJ career around <strong>2013</strong> and has since become a prominent figure in the global trance community. She hosts and programs several high-profile radio shows, including:</p><ul><li><strong>Kaleidoscope.OUT OF TIME</strong> — her flagship psytrance show, broadcast exclusively on <strong>DI.FM</strong> (Digital Impulse) Goa-Psy Trance channel, airing monthly on the last Friday</li><li><strong>ENTRANCE</strong> — a progressive trance series exclusively on <strong>Afterhours.FM</strong> (AH.FM), airing monthly on the second Tuesday at 3 PM Amsterdam time</li><li><strong>ReBirth.The Future Is Now!</strong> — her uplifting/emotional trance show, featured on stations like <strong>93.1 Play-FM</strong> (USA, Florida), <strong>Discover Trance Radio</strong>, and others</li></ul><p>Her mixes and collaborations (often with artists like DJ Lucky, InWinter, Phil Langham, CASW, and Wolmy Balmart) have been broadcast on numerous international platforms, including <strong>Play Trance Radio</strong>, <strong>Tempo-Radio Mexico</strong>, <strong>Trance Athens Radio</strong>, <strong>Trance World</strong>, <strong>Beats2dance Trance Family</strong>, <strong>Trance-Energy Radio</strong>, <strong>Radio-Trance-Nation</strong>, <strong>Lumix FM</strong>, <strong>Phoenix Trance Radio</strong>, <strong>XFM Romania</strong>, <strong>Trance Is Star</strong> (Ukraine), and more. She has also delivered special sets like <strong>EOYC</strong> (End of Year Countdown) on AH.FM and various guest mixes.</p><h3>Productions &amp; label work</h3><p>Through <strong>AVIVmedia</strong>, Jenny Karol supports and releases trance music, focusing on uplifting, emotional, progressive, tech, and psy subgenres. Her own mixes frequently feature her original edits, collaborations, and high-energy track selections that blend vocal elements, powerful builds, and euphoric drops. Her content is widely available on <strong>SoundCloud</strong> (@jenny_karol), <strong>Mixcloud</strong> (avivmedia), <strong>Deezer</strong>, <strong>Apple Podcasts</strong>, <strong>YouTube</strong>, and other streaming services.</p><p>Promos for radio shows can be sent to <a href="mailto:jenny.avivmedia@gmail.com"><strong>jenny.avivmedia@gmail.com</strong></a>.</p><h3>Style &amp; vibe</h3><p>Jenny Karol's sound is characterized by <strong>uplifting</strong>, <strong>emotional</strong>, and <strong>energetic</strong> trance vibes, often incorporating progressive and psy elements for immersive, festival-ready journeys. Her sets evoke hope, euphoria, and a sense of rebirth — themes reflected in her show titles like <strong>ReBirth.The Future Is Now!</strong>.</p><p>Still very active in 2026, she continues to broadcast regularly, release new episodes, and grow the trance scene through her label and global radio presence.</p><p><strong>Quote often linked to her vibe:</strong> « You Were Never Born and You Will Never Die — Kaleidoscope moves us all in turn. »</p><p>A dedicated trance ambassador and label visionary, <strong>Jenny Karol</strong> brings emotional depth, powerful progressions, and pure uplifting energy to the worldwide dancefloor — from psy shadows to euphoric peaks.</p>
