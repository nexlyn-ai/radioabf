						<h2>ABF CLUB // SATURDAYS GUEST MIX</h2><p>&nbsp;</p><p><strong>Mikel Cugga</strong> (also stylized as <strong>MiKel CuGGa</strong> or <strong>Mikel.Cugga</strong>) is a <strong>German DJ, producer</strong>, and <strong>electronic music enthusiast</strong> based in <strong>Cologne</strong> (and previously associated with Berlin), Germany. With roots tracing back to the late 1990s rave scene, he has built a career focused on <strong>deep house</strong>, <strong>tech house</strong>, <strong>jacking house</strong>, <strong>garage house</strong>, <strong>disco house</strong>, <strong>funky house</strong>, <strong>bassline house</strong>, and <strong>underground</strong> electronic sounds.</p><h3>Early beginnings &amp; influences</h3><p>Mikel Cugga's passion for electronic music ignited in <strong>1998</strong> at the legendary <strong>Love Parade</strong>, where the powerful beats of house and techno first captured him. From that moment, he knew he wanted to create and share <strong>housemusic</strong>. He began producing and DJing in the early 2000s, evolving into a versatile artist who blends groovy, dancefloor-oriented tracks with underground edge.</p><h3>Productions &amp; discography</h3><p>As a <strong>producer</strong>, Mikel Cugga has released numerous tracks and EPs on various international labels, including <strong>Sunrising Records</strong>, <strong>Re:vibe Music</strong>, <strong>SonarWave Records</strong>, and compilations like <strong>Future House Sensations</strong> and <strong>Selective: Tech House</strong>. Standout releases include:</p><ul><li><strong>Groove Shaker Boogie EP</strong> (Mikel Cugga Club Mix, 2018)</li><li><strong>Chicago Groove</strong> (Original Mix, 2019/2022)</li><li><strong>Bienvenido a Mi Club</strong> (Extended Mix, 2019)</li><li><strong>Tacos House</strong> (Single)</li><li>Appearances in compilations alongside artists like Pasadena Beat, Curol, Warp Brothers, and more</li></ul><p>His tracks are available on <strong>Beatport</strong>, <strong>Spotify</strong> (with a dedicated following), <strong>Apple Music</strong>, <strong>Deezer</strong>, and other platforms, often charting in tech house and house categories.</p><h3>DJ sets &amp; collaborations</h3><p>Mikel Cugga is active as a <strong>DJ</strong>, delivering high-energy sets and back-to-back (B2B) sessions. Notable collaborations include:</p><ul><li>B2B with <strong>DJ Dragon1965</strong> ("BOOGIE To THe BAM BAM")</li><li>B2B with <strong>VIK BENNO</strong> on <strong>House Fusion Radio</strong></li><li>B2B with<strong> Betty Mix (BETTY71) </strong>("BACK 2 DISCO")</li><li>Regular mixes shared on <strong>Mixcloud</strong> (mikelcugga / mikel-cugga3), <strong>SoundCloud</strong> (@ielua / mikel-cugga), and other platforms</li></ul><p>He has been affiliated with radio networks like <strong>DE3P RADIO NETWORK</strong> and <strong>ProMoRecords</strong>, contributing to the deep/tech/jacking house scene through guest mixes and live broadcasts.</p><h3>Style &amp; presence</h3><p>Mikel Cugga's sound is all about <strong>groovy, funky, and club-ready</strong> vibes — from classic disco influences to modern tech house and bass-driven underground grooves. He remains a consistent contributor to the European electronic scene, with a focus on quality productions and energetic performances.</p><p>Still active in 2026, Mikel Cugga continues to release fresh tracks, drop mixes, and collaborate across platforms, keeping the housemusic spirit alive since the Love Parade days.</p><p><strong>Quote often linked to his vibe:</strong> « From the Love Parade beats of '98 to today's dancefloor — housemusic forever! »</p><p>A dedicated German house ambassador, <strong>Mikel Cugga</strong> brings deep grooves, jackin energy, and underground soul to every production and set.</p>
