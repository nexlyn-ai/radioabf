LP Remix is a UK-based DJ and remixer active in the electronic music scene, particularly known for his high-energy sets and explosive remixes on Radio ABF (Alternative Bass Fréquence).

Specializing in dance, commercial house, big room, EDM remixes, and upbeat club sounds, LP Remix transforms current pop hits, classics, and trending tracks into powerful dancefloor weapons. His style features massive builds, killer drops, seamless transitions, and a signature personal twist that makes every mix instantly addictive and festival-ready.

Career & radio presence

LP Remix has become one of the most dynamic and prolific contributors to Radio ABF, where he regularly guests on the ABF Club concept.
