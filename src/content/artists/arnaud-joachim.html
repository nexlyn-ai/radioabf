ABF CLUB // SATURDAYS GUEST MIX

 

Arnaud Joachim (also known as DJ Arnaud Joachim) is a French DJ and electronic music selector based in Villefranche-sur-Saône, France. An active contributor to the international webradio and online DJ scene, he is recognized for his energetic, eclectic mixes that span electronic, dance, house, and broader e-music genres.

Career & style

Arnaud Joachim has established himself through regular guest appearances and residencies on global webradios, particularly on Radio Trip Hop (Brazil), where he became a resident DJ starting around early 2024. His shows air weekly (often Wednesdays at 4pm São Paulo / 8-9pm Paris time), featuring high-quality selections of electronic music, DJ sets, and promotional mixes that highlight the best in contemporary e-music from around the world.

He also maintains a long-standing association with OnlyForPromo (a platform for resident DJs) and earlier episodes on Mixcloud and SoundCloud (under arnaud-joachim-380293532). These mixes showcase his ability to curate engaging, crowd-pleasing sets with strong transitions, groovy vibes, and a focus on uplifting electronic sounds.

His style is versatile and festival-friendly, blending dance, electro, house, and other electronic subgenres to create immersive listening experiences perfect for home sessions or pre-parties.

Media presence & activities

Arnaud Joachim shares his work primarily through:
