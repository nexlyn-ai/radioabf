						<h2>ABF CLUB // SATURDAYS GUEST MIX</h2><p>&nbsp;</p><p><strong>DJ Ari's Style</strong> (real name <strong>Sylvain Arini</strong>) is a <strong>Franco-Italian DJ, remixer</strong>, and <strong>electronic music selector</strong> based on the <strong>French Riviera</strong> (Nice area, South of France). A self-taught artist with a vibrant, colorful energy inspired by the lively Mediterranean coast, he has been active since <strong>2015</strong> and is known for his eclectic house-focused sets blending <strong>deep house</strong>, <strong>tech house</strong>, <strong>soulful house</strong>, <strong>chill</strong>, <strong>progressive deep house</strong>, <strong>funky house</strong>, <strong>Black/R'n'B vocals</strong>, and timeless <strong>house classics</strong>.</p><h3>Career &amp; beginnings</h3><p>DJ Ari's Style kicked off his career at <strong>SBS ANIMATION</strong> in the iconic <strong>"La Piscine"</strong> nightclub in <strong>Auron</strong> (a popular ski resort in the French Alps), where he honed his skills in high-energy club environments. From the 2010s onward, he established himself as one of the South of France's go-to DJs, playing in clubs, resorts, and events while developing a signature style that mixes underground freshness with nostalgic gold tracks.</p><p>During the <strong>2020 lockdown</strong>, he seized the opportunity to become a <strong>radio host</strong> and <strong>DJ-president</strong>, using online platforms to keep people happy and dancing from home. He introduced listeners to the latest <strong>underground deep house</strong>, <strong>chill</strong>, and <strong>soulful</strong> productions while never forgetting his roots in <strong>funky</strong>, <strong>Black vocal</strong>, and <strong>classic house</strong> gems. This period marked a turning point, with regular radio shows, podcasts, and collaborations.</p><p>He has since become a recurring guest and contributor on international webradios such as <strong>JDK Radio</strong> (UK-based, with sessions like TECH HOUSE Jams, TECHNO MELODIC PROGRESSIVE, Universe Sessions, and more), <strong>Radio Trip Hop</strong>, and others, where he shares live mixes, seasonal specials (e.g., Summer Tech House EP 01 2025), and themed sets like <strong>Deep Oriental</strong>, <strong>Deep Day</strong>, and <strong>Best House Music</strong>.</p><h3>Productions &amp; remixes</h3><p>As a <strong>remixer</strong>, DJ Ari's Style creates personal edits and reworkings, including notable ones like <strong>Stromae remix sets</strong>, <strong>"Color Blind" by Bono</strong> (progressive deep house remix, 2025), and various house-oriented bootlegs. His mixes often feature a global, worldly vibe (e.g., "The Whole Earth Listening to Deep House" series), reflecting his love for diverse influences.</p><p>His content is widely shared on platforms like <strong>SoundCloud</strong> (under dj ari's style / sylvainarini), <strong>Bandcamp</strong>, <strong>HearThis.at</strong>, <strong>Mixcloud</strong>, and <strong>Spinnin' Records Talent Pool</strong>, with regular uploads of full sets, live recordings, and podcast-style episodes.</p><h3>Style &amp; vibe</h3><p>DJ Ari's Style delivers <strong>groovy, uplifting, and feel-good</strong> electronic music that bridges underground depth with accessible dancefloor energy. His sets are perfect for summer vibes, après-ski parties, or relaxed home listening — always aiming to make people smile and move.</p><p><strong>Quote often linked to his vibe:</strong> « House music that makes you happy — from the Riviera to the world! »</p><p>A passionate Franco-Italian DJ from Nice, <strong>DJ Ari's Style</strong> (Sylvain Arini) continues to spread positive grooves, underground discoveries, and timeless house spirit through his radio shows, remixes, and energetic sets in 2026.</p>
