Laurent Schark is a French DJ, producer, remixer, sound engineer, and record label manager based in London since many years. Active in the electronic music scene for over three decades, he is recognized as one of the most respected and influential French artists in the UK capital.

Career & style

Laurent Schark began DJing in 1994, started appearing on radio in 1995, and turned professional in music production around 1999. He specializes in vocal house, electro house, disco house, funky house, nu disco, and French Touch-inspired sounds, often delivering high-energy, feel-good productions with a distinctive "Schark" signature.

He has been repeatedly voted among the best DJs in London by major publications such as Time Out and The Guardian, and is widely regarded by the London nightlife press as the best French DJ in the city since 2009.

His DJ performances stand out for their live hybrid approach: combining CDs, vinyl, hardware samplers, and keyboards (including keytar) into unique, powerful live acts that go beyond standard club sets.

Productions & label

Laurent is the founder and Managing Director of Dominium Recordings, a label focused on house music variations (funky, disco, vocal, etc.). He has an extensive discography including originals, remixes, and collaborations:

  • Early works: "Movin'" feat. Jodie Cooper, "My Destination" feat. Amy Lauren Angel
  • Notable tracks: "Chemistry", "New Reason" feat. Darren Ellison (multiple versions/remixes), "One Of A Kind"
  • Collaborations: With artists like Chris Willis, Zeb Atlas, Afro Medusa (remix), Moussa Clarke, and many more
  • Various anthems and releases on labels like Evolusound, Night Dance Records, Funkytronik Music, Karmic Power Records, etc.

Radio & media presence

Laurent Schark is one of the most syndicated radio personalities in house music. His flagship program, "The Laurent Schark Selection", is broadcast weekly on dozens of stations worldwide. Since 2015, the monthly "Laurent Schark Selection LIVE SHOW" has become the world's most syndicated live house radio program, reaching over 40 radios and millions of unique listeners.

He remains very active in 2026, regularly releasing new shows, mixes, and updates via platforms like Mixcloud, Linktree, and social media.

Quote often associated with his vibe: Positive, groovy, and timeless house music that makes you move and smile.

A true pillar of vocal/funky house with a strong French identity, Laurent Schark continues to bridge clubs, festivals, radio waves, and studios with passion and consistency after more than 30 years in the game.
