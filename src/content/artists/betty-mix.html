Co-Founder, Manager & DJ – Head of DJs & Communication Radio ABF

Betty Mix (other name Betty Elisabeth Snoopy) is a French DJ and passionate music lover, known for her energetic and eclectic online radio sets. Based in France (with activity around Charnay-lès-Mâcon and broader French scenes), she combines her career as a hospital nurse with a deep dedication to DJing and sharing music.

Career & style

Betty Mix has been active in the digital DJ scene for several years, regularly broadcasting live mixes on various webradios. Her style is versatile and feel-good, psytrance, trance, progressive, melodic techno, funky, house and upbeat electronic vibes designed to get people moving. She emphasizes fun, positive energy, and long mixes that keep the dancefloor alive.

She is particularly known for her recurring resident slots and guest appearances, including:

  • Weekly/resident shows on Radio ABF (@abfonair), where she delivers her signature Betty Mix series (e.g., Mix #010 in January 2026)
  • Appearances on House Fusion Radio, with regular guest sets (e.g., December 2025 shows)
  • Contributions to other platforms and concepts like Infinity Mix

Her mixes are consistently uploaded to Mixcloud under Betty71, where she shares full broadcasts with tracklists, allowing fans to relisten and discover new music.

Media presence & activities

Betty Mix maintains an active online presence:

  • X/Twitter (@BETTY071): Regular announcements of upcoming and archived sets, with a warm, community-focused tone ("Happy Sunday!", "Big Love!")
  • Mixcloud (mixcloud.com/betty71): Home to her full DJ sets and series
  • Facebook (Betty Mix page): Additional updates and fan interaction 

As of January 2026, she continues to produce fresh content weekly, inviting listeners to tune in live and catch the replays. Her bio often features the motto "Music Is My Alibi ♫", reflecting her passion for music as both escape and expression.

A dedicated and consistent figure in the French webradio and online DJ community, Betty Mix brings joy, groove, and non-stop energy to her listeners — proving that passion for music shines through regardless of the spotlight.
