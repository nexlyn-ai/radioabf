ABF CLUB // Every Monday 22h CET (= 21h UTC)

 

JNOES (Facebook pseudonym Jean Noes) is a French DJ active in the webradio and club scene, best known as a resident DJ for the ABF CLUB concept on Radio ABF.

Specializing in upbeat dance, house, club, drum'n'bass, and electro sounds, JNOES delivers energetic, festive, and dancefloor-oriented sets, with a strong focus on current remixes, reworked classics, and powerful build-ups that keep the energy rising.

Career & residency

JNOES has established himself as a regular and beloved figure on Radio ABF, a French webradio dedicated to electronic music (house, dance, electro, club). As a resident DJ of the ABF CLUB concept, he hosts recurring live shows and broadcasts, typically in the evenings or on weekends, offering long, dynamic, and accessible mixes perfect for pre-parties or home clubbing sessions.

His appearances are highly followed by the Radio ABF listener community, who appreciate his positive, groovy, and no-fuss style. He regularly shares his sets on Mixcloud, Facebook (under the name Jean Noes), and other platforms, with live announcements and replay links.

Style & vibe

JNOES’s sound is typically:

  • Groovy and house
  • Drum'n'bass
  • Dance / Big Room influences
  • Pop-dance and EDM-friendly remixes
  • Always delivered with an uplifting and festive energy

He is part of the new generation of webradio DJs keeping the online club mix culture alive, creating direct connections with listeners through live chats and social media.

Still active as of January 2026, JNOES continues to light up the Radio ABF airwaves with his ABF CLUB sessions, proving that passion for the dancefloor knows no limits — even from a personal setup.
