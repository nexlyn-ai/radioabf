DEGIANNY (stylized as DEGIANNY or Dj DEGIANNY) is a DJ, artist, producer, composer, and multi-instrumentalist active in the electronic music scene. He specializes in Progressive House, House, Melodic Techno, Trance, EDM, and Electronics, blending melodic elements with powerful builds and emotional piano touches.

Originally associated with Bucharest, Romania, and having lived in places like Cambridge, UK, he has been based in Arrecife, Lanzarote (Canary Islands, Spain) in recent years, where he proudly represents Spain in international rankings.

Career & background

With over 25 years of experience as a pianist and keyboardist — having played in various pop and rock bands — DEGIANNY transitioned into electronic music production and DJing. He has been active as a DJ for around 10+ years (with consistent presence since the mid-2010s), combining live keyboard skills, piano melodies, and DJ mixing.

He gained significant recognition through ReverbNation charts, where he ranked in the Top 10 Best DJs in the World for multiple years (2014–2019) and claimed the #1 spot in Spain from 2015 onward, even reaching World #1 DJ status in 2020–2021 according to the platform.

DEGIANNY is known for his live performances and streaming sessions — often emphasizing real mixing without sync, markers, or pre-production — delivering high-energy sets in melodic techno, progressive house, trance, and more. He frequently hosts YouTube live sessions (Sunday Sunset Playlists, Massive Trance Sessions, Melodic Techno Nights) and shares content on TikTok, Facebook, and Instagram.

Productions & style

As a music producer and composer, DEGIANNY releases original tracks, remixes, edits, and collaborations. Notable works include:

  • EQUILY (Original Mix - Radio Edit)
  • Going On (Original Mix)
  • Unstoppable (Original Mix)
  • Beautiful Time (ft. Alexandru Dutulescu)
  • Hello, Crypto (Original Mix)
  • FURY (upcoming/teased)
  • Remixes/edits: Mika - Relax, Take It Easy, Symphony (Clean Bandit ft. Zara Larsson - Hardwell Remix edit), KSHMR & Tigerlily - Invisible Children, and more

His sound often features emotional piano melodies, uplifting progressions, and festival-ready energy, with a strong emphasis on connecting with the audience.

Media presence & activities

  • YouTube (@degianny): Main channel for lives, premieres, and full mixes
  • SoundCloud (degianny-official): Tracks and releases
  • Facebook (djdegianny): Over 13K likes, regular updates
  • ReverbNation: Core platform for his chart success and music uploads

He continues to be very active in 2026, regularly streaming live sets, releasing new music, and engaging with fans through social media and online performances.

Quote often linked to his vibe: « Music has always been the biggest passion in my life. It’s about a way of living. » (DEGIANNY)

A passionate, self-taught multi-instrumentalist turned electronic force, DEGIANNY brings soulful melodies and raw energy to the global scene — still dropping heat from the Canary Islands.
