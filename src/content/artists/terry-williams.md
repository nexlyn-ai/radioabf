						<h2>ABF CLUB // SATURDAYS GUEST MIX</h2><p>&nbsp;</p><p><strong>Terry Williams</strong> is a <strong>French DJ and producer</strong> specializing in <strong>house</strong>, <strong>deep house</strong>, <strong>tech house</strong>, and <strong>techno</strong> sounds. Based in France, he is a dedicated figure in the electronic music underground, known for his groovy, soulful, and club-oriented productions that blend classic house influences with modern energy.</p><h3>Career &amp; beginnings</h3><p>Terry Williams began his musical journey as a <strong>DJ</strong>, performing in clubs and building his skills over several years. As production gradually took precedence over pure DJing, he shifted focus to creating original tracks and remixes. In <strong>2008</strong>, he co-founded his first label, <strong>Reka Prika</strong>, alongside producers <strong>Reka Ference</strong>, <strong>Kaprika</strong>, and <strong>Jon Maes</strong>, marking an important milestone in his career.</p><p>He has since released numerous tracks on respected house and techno labels, including:</p><ul><li><strong>Jango Music</strong></li><li><strong>Orgasmixxx</strong></li><li><strong>Jays Records</strong></li><li><strong>Disco Balls Records</strong></li><li>And others such as <strong>LAD Publishing &amp; Records</strong> (e.g., albums like <strong>"Midnight"</strong> and <strong>"Iberican Origin"</strong>)</li></ul><p>His discography features originals and remixes in <strong>house</strong>, <strong>tekhouse</strong>, <strong>techno</strong>, <strong>vocal house</strong>, <strong>soulful house</strong>, and <strong>Iberican house</strong> styles, with collaborations and support from artists like <strong>Miss Luna</strong> (Ibizaliveradio), <strong>Natalino Nunes</strong>, <strong>Nicolas Francoual</strong>, and <strong>DJ Escobar</strong> (B2B sessions on Xlarge Radio).</p><p>In <strong>2018</strong>, he launched a second label project, <strong>Peneloop Records</strong>, with producer <strong>Jon Maes</strong>, focusing on underground <strong>house</strong> and <strong>techno</strong> releases.</p><h3>Style &amp; presence</h3><p>Terry Williams' sound is deeply rooted in <strong>house music</strong> with strong influences from <strong>vocal</strong>, <strong>soulful</strong>, <strong>tek</strong>, and <strong>Iberican</strong> grooves. His DJ sets are immersive and dancefloor-focused, often featuring a mix of current productions, classics, and personal edits that keep the energy high and the vibe positive.</p><p>His music is available on major platforms:</p><ul><li><strong>Beatport</strong> and <strong>Traxsource</strong> (for downloads and charts)</li><li><strong>SoundCloud</strong> (@terry-williams) – where he shares tracks, mixes, and full sets</li><li><strong>Spotify</strong> (≈151 monthly listeners)</li><li><strong>YouTube</strong> (Terry Williams Producer channel)</li></ul><p>He has also appeared on international webradios, including <strong>Radio XLarge</strong> (with dedicated shows as Terry Williams France).</p><p>Still active in 2026, Terry Williams continues to produce fresh material, share new mixes, and contribute to the house scene with consistency and passion for groovy electronic sounds.</p><p><strong>Quote often linked to his vibe:</strong> « House music forever — deep, soulful, and ready to move the floor. »</p><p>A true house devotee from France, <strong>Terry Williams</strong> delivers timeless grooves, underground edge, and pure dancefloor energy through his productions and sets.</p>
