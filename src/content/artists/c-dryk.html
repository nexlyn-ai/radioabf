C-Dryk (also stylized C•Dryk™) is a French DJ, producer, remixer, and Pioneer DJ demonstrator originally from Metz, Lorraine, France, and currently based in Charleroi, Belgium.

Active in the electronic music scene since the mid-1990s, he began his passion for DJing at the age of 16. He has built a versatile career blending club performances, productions, live demonstrations, and media appearances across France, Belgium, Luxembourg, and Germany.

Early years & background

Born in Lorraine, France, C-Dryk was introduced to professional deejaying techniques, animation, sound, and lighting between 1993 and 1998 by renowned professionals. He participated in turntable-based DJ competitions, including the French selection for the DMC World DJ Championships in 1997.

From the late 1990s to mid-2000s (initially known as DJ Deç’1), he performed at numerous clubs in northeastern France (New-Max, Sunshine, Silver Night, etc.) and cultural/student events at major engineering schools (ENIM, ENPC, ENSAM). During this period, he also worked with professional sound and lighting companies and began producing tracks as a ghost producer.

Since 2016, he has been a key Pioneer DJ demonstrator, showcasing CDJ, DJM, controllers, and other gear in stores, bars, and clubs across multiple countries.

Style & productions

C-Dryk is a versatile artist whose sets and productions span a wide range of house subgenres and beyond:

  • Core styles: NuDisco, Jackin House, Deep House, Progressive House, Electro House, Future House, Dance, Big Room
  • Additional vibes: Old school, R&B, Reggaeton, Groove, Funk

He has released original tracks and remixes on various labels, including recent highlights such as:

  • The Jackin’ Code (Jackin House, featured and reviewed positively in 2025)
  • Set Me Free (on Cruise Music, gaining attention in the disco-jackin' scene)
  • Jack My Disco (on Panam Vibes Records, 2025)
  • Other titles: Till the Break of Day, Paris Got the Groove, My Funky Way, and the album Funky Edm Is Life!

His music is available on platforms like Spotify, Apple Music, SoundCloud, YouTube, and Shazam. He has also produced notable remixes (e.g., DJ Bobo – Everybody).

Activities & media presence

C-Dryk is very active in the radio and online space:

  • Regular guest on webradios like Clubsoundz Lille, Clubtronic Radio (with series like “C-Dryk Scratch Sessions”)
  • Hosts/presents shows featuring Energy House B3B collaborations and scratch/mix techniques
  • Strong social media presence (Facebook, X/Twitter @cdryk_, etc.) with regular live set announcements and updates

He continues to perform in bars, clubs, and events in the Grand-Est region of France, Belgium, and Luxembourg, while maintaining a focus on groovy, feel-good house music.

Quote often linked to his vibe: « One Love! ❤️ » (frequently used in his posts and bio)

A dedicated figure in the cross-border French-Belgian house scene, C-Dryk combines technical skill, groove-oriented productions, and a long-standing passion for the craft — still very active and releasing fresh material in 2026.
