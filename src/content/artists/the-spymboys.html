The Spymboys (alias SPYMBOYS, real name Yohan Stasia) is a French DJ and producer based in Savoie, in the French Alps. A passionate music lover and multi-genre selector, he specializes in high-energy, feel-good electronic sounds, blending electro house, disco house, tech house, bass house, deep house, melodic house, progressive house, future house, Latin house, funky house, jackin house, indie dance, electro pop/dance, techno, trance, hip hop US/old school, reggaeton, and moombahton.

Early beginnings & career

As a teenager, The Spymboys started mixing for student parties, quickly honing his skills in the vibrant French party scene. He then spent several seasons working as a resident DJ in the trendy clubs of the Savoie winter resorts (French Alps), where he played to enthusiastic crowds of skiers, locals, and tourists. This experience shaped his signature style: festive, uplifting sets full of groove, good vibes, and crowd-pleasing energy.

Over the years, he has become a versatile DJ performer known for creating memorable events through his project Spym Events by The Spymboys. His mixes are designed to bring smiles, class, and non-stop dancing to any occasion.

Radio residencies & online presence

The Spymboys is an active resident DJ on several international webradios, including:

  • Radio ABF (Paris)
  • House Fusion Radio (London)

He regularly delivers guest mixes, resident shows, and themed sets (e.g., Mental Transition, Afro House Kingdom, Underground Rivers, Behind the Mirror, Digital Tech House, EDM Platinum Room, Pure Jackin House, Africa Voices, Yconyx Integral).

His content is widely shared on platforms like Mixcloud (under The Spymboys / Yohan Stasia), SoundCloud (@spymboys), and X/Twitter (@TheSpymboys), where he posts fresh mixes, announcements, and collaborations (e.g., with VIPpassNY on funky/disco house sessions).

Style & vibe

The Spymboys' sets are always festive and energizing, with a focus on quality selections, seamless transitions, and a perfect balance between current hits, remixes, and timeless grooves. Whether deep and melodic, punchy and bass-heavy, or sunny and tropical, his music transports listeners straight to the dancefloor — from Alpine après-ski parties to virtual global sessions.

Still very active in 2026, he continues to drop new mixes, participate in contests (e.g., Decoded Magazine submissions), and spread his "montagne electro" sound worldwide.
